ABTalks · 60 Days Claude Challenge
Day 03
Role-Based Prompting
"The same question. Different roles. Different perspectives."
The Core Question
How can I build a successful AI career in the next 2 years?
No Role
Generic Response
Prompt
"How can I build a successful AI career…?"
Output Highlights
General career advice
Broad recommendations
Limited personalization
Founder Persona
Strategic Perspective
Prompt
"You are a startup founder who built AI companies…"
Output Highlights
Networking strategies
Business opportunities
Personal branding
Value creation mindset
Developer Persona
Technical Roadmap
Prompt
"You are a senior AI developer and engineer…"
Output Highlights
Technical roadmap
Projects & portfolio
Programming skills
Industry tools & practices
Key Learning
Assigning a role changes the AI's perspective and produces more targeted, relevant, and useful responses.
